About Us

Founded in 2001, MediCare started as a small family-owned heatlhcare store in Kemanggisan, Jakarta. As more and more people get to know about MediCare,it is natural for us to expand out stores to other cities and provinces. Now, we have more than 100 stores spread out in almost every major cities in Indonesia, including but not limited to Aceh, Medan, Palembang, Jakarta, Surabaya, Pontianak, and Makassar. We also offers a wider variety for products from healthcare, cosmetics, medicines, and many more. We have commitment to stay true to our humble origin and serving our customers with best customer services and high quality products.
